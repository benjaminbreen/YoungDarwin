# Lawson House Framed Prints

Public-domain scans from Wikimedia Commons, downscaled to 1280px for runtime
use. Applied by `scripts/blender_build_lawson_house.py` as framed wall art in
the office/library.

- `gillray-plumb-pudding.jpg` — James Gillray, *The Plumb-pudding in Danger*
  (1805), hand-coloured etching. Source: Commons
  `Caricature gillray plumpudding.jpg`.
- `gillray-slippy-weather.jpg` — James Gillray, *Very Slippy-Weather* (1808),
  hand-coloured etching. Source: Commons `Very slippy-weather.jpg`.
- `thornton-cereus.jpg` — Robert Thornton, *The Night-Blowing Cereus* from
  *The Temple of Flora* (1800). Source: Commons `Night-blowing cereus.jpg`.
- `thornton-carnations.jpg` — Robert Thornton, *A Group of Carnations* from
  *The Temple of Flora* (1803). Source: Commons
  `The Temple of Flora, Carnations.jpg`.
