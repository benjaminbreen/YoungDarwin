# HMS Beagle Cabin Textures

Runtime PBR textures used by `scripts/blender_build_hms_beagle_cabin.py`.
The generated cabin GLB embeds the textures, so these files are retained as
the reproducible source material for future cabin rebuilds.

## Period Maps

### FitzRoy Galapagos survey

- Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Admiralty_Chart_No_1375_Galapagos_Islands,_Published_1841_(cropped).jpg
- Creator: Hydrographic Office of the Admiralty, after the survey by Robert
  FitzRoy and the officers of HMS Beagle in 1835-36.
- License: Public domain (UK Government work).
- Runtime file: `maps/fitzroy-galapagos-chart.jpg`.
- Historical note: the surviving finished chart was published in 1841. Its
  in-game tabletop use represents the survey sheet being developed aboard the
  Beagle rather than a literal copy of the later printed plate.

### Arrowsmith world map

- Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Arrowsmith%27s_map_of_the_world_(1812).jpg
- Creators: Aaron Arrowsmith and Samuel Lewis; published by Thomas & Andrews.
- Date: 1812.
- License: Public domain.
- Runtime file: `maps/arrowsmith-world-map.jpg`.
